# Astra Mekanika — Player Wiki

## Quick Start
You spawn with leather armor, wooden tools, 24 bread, 32 torches, a Waystone, and the **Astra Mekanika Field Guide** (Patchouli book). Open the quest book (default: `K`) to begin Phase I.

---

## The Five Phases

### Phase I — The Awakening
**Goal:** Build an industrial foundation with Immersive Engineering.  
**Key mods:** Immersive Engineering · Ars Nouveau · Create · Tinkers' Construct · Thermal Expansion  
**Phase seal:** Rusted Conduit Core  
> Craft the Rusted Conduit Core (IE Steel + Source Gem + Thermal RF Coil + Treated Wood) to unlock Phase II.

Milestones:
- Build a Coke Oven and Blast Furnace to produce Steel
- Set up a basic LV power grid (Waterwheel → Kinetic Dynamo)
- Craft an Ars Nouveau Apprentice Spellbook
- Enchant your gear (Enchanting Table + 15 bookshelves)

### Phase II — Circuits & Sorcery
**Goal:** Enter Blood Magic and set up early digital storage.  
**Key mods:** Blood Magic · Botania · Refined Storage · Immersive Engineering (HV)  
**Phase seal:** Crimson Circuit of Passage  
> Craft the Crimson Circuit (Blaze Powder + IE Wire Coil + Weak Blood Orb + Blank Rune) to unlock Phase III.

Milestones:
- Build a Blood Magic Altar and fill your first Weak Blood Orb
- Set up a Botania Mana Pool and Mana Spreader
- Build a Refined Storage Controller for your first digital storage network
- Upgrade your IE power to MV/HV tier

### Phase III — The Forest Beyond
**Goal:** Conquer the Twilight Forest and unlock Mekanism.  
**Key mods:** Twilight Forest · Mekanism · Mystical Agriculture · Better Nether/End  
**Phase seal:** Twilight Sigil Plate  
> Defeat Naga, Twilight Lich, Hydra, and Snow Queen. Craft the Twilight Sigil Plate to unlock Phase IV.

Milestones:
- Enter the Twilight Forest through a leaf-framed portal over a 2×2 water pool
- Defeat Twilight bosses in order: Naga → Lich → Hydra → Snow Queen (Knight Phantoms optional)
- Build a Mekanism Metallurgic Infuser (unlocked by the Twilight Sigil Plate)
- Set up 5× ore processing with Mekanism

### Phase IV — Infinite Architecture
**Goal:** Build an AE2 ME network and a NuclearCraft fission reactor.  
**Key mods:** Applied Energistics 2 · NuclearCraft · Psi · Ad Astra · Cataclysm  
**Phase seal:** Reactor Authority Seal  
> Defeat the Ignited Revenant (Flame Eye) and Ender Golem (Void Eye) in Cataclysm. Craft the Reactor Authority Seal.

Milestones:
- Build a full AE2 ME Controller and channel network
- Construct and run a NuclearCraft Fission Reactor
- Defeat the Cataclysm bosses for their Eye drops
- Explore Ad Astra rocket content

### Phase V — The Architect's Ascent
**Goal:** Master Draconic Evolution and assemble the Heart of the Vault.  
**Key mods:** Draconic Evolution · Blood Magic (Master tier) · Mekanism (Atomic Alloy)  
**Phase seal:** Heart of the Vault  
> Craft the Heart of the Vault (AE2 Fluix + DE Draconium + Master Blood Orb + Flame Eye + Atomic Alloy).

Milestones:
- Build a Draconic Evolution Fusion Crafting Core (requires Heart of the Vault)
- Obtain Awakened Draconium (fuse Draconium in Fusion Crafter)
- Assemble the **Vault Convergence** (Awakened Draconium × 4 + Dragon Egg + Master Blood Orb × 2 + Heart of the Vault + Atomic Alloy)

---

## Endgame: Prestige

Completing Vault Convergence unlocks the **Prestige** chapter. Submit your **Prestige Seal** to the prestige altar to ascend:
- **Prestige I — Awakened:** Mob difficulty increases. Escalating coin rewards.
- **Prestige II — Ascended:** Further difficulty and reward scaling.
- **Prestige III — Transcendent:** Maximum difficulty. Legendary status.

## Blood Moon

Every 7th in-game night, the sky turns red at dusk. Mob spawn rates increase and waves of undead appear near every player. **Sleeping is disabled during a Blood Moon.** Survive until dawn.

## Tips

- **Waystones:** Place one at your base immediately. You start with one in your hotbar.
- **Tinkers' tools:** Don't neglect them — a Tinkers' pickaxe with Efficiency + Fortune outperforms iron until mid-Phase II.
- **Blood Magic:** Requires your own HP to fill the altar. Keep golden apples nearby.
- **Twilight Forest:** Bring Fire Resistance potions. The Hydra's heads breathe fire.
- **Mekanism ore processing:** Set up the full chain (Crusher → Enrichment Chamber → Smelter) for 5× output before proceeding to Phase IV.
- **AE2 channels:** Each Controller face provides 32 channels. Plan your cable layout before building.
- **Saint's Dragons:** Cindervane spawns in deserts. Bring Fire Resistance. Tame with 6–8 raw chicken.
