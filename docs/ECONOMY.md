## Key Insights

1. **Prestige Dominates Supply:** The endgame prestige chapter provides 355 coins (44% of all coins), the largest single source.

2. **Side Content is Substantial:** Side quests provide 279 coins (34% of total), making them essential for players who want to access higher-tier shop items.

3. **Shop is a Sink:** The economy is tuned so players **cannot** buy everything. This creates meaningful choices:
   - Early game items (5-30 coins) are freely available
   - Mid-tier items (35-80 coins) require selective questing
   - Prestige items (100+ coins) demand commitment to side content

4. **Phase Distribution:**
   - Phases I-IV are lean on coins (107 total), focused on progression
   - Phase V & Prestige are coin-rich (426 total), rewarding completion
   - Players reaching endgame will have earned most coins

5. **Recommended Investment Paths:**
   - Minimal spend: Core items only (Bread, Obsidian, XP)
   - Standard: Add useful resources (Gold, Diamonds, Steel)
   - Prestige: Pursue blade, armor, and Nether Star
   - Ultimate: All items require 100% completion of all chapters + side content
